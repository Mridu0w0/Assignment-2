Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Kindergarten is for children between the ages of 3 and 6. It's a time when children develop their social, cognitive, and physical skills, and learn how to be independent.

Elementary School

Elementary school age is typically between 5 and 11 years old, depending on the school system. Elementary school is also known as primary education.

Middle (10-16) Years

The years between 10 and 16 are part of adolescence, which is the period of transition between childhood and adulthood. During this time, children experience rapid physical, cognitive, and psychosocial growth.
